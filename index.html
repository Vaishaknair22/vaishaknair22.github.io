Martian Depth Map Generation

At a national hackathon by the Government of India, our team developed a depth mapping solution for Mars' Valle Marineris using ISRO's Mars Orbitor Mission data. We created a 1200-image dataset from NASA, JAXA, and ESA satellite imagery, implementing ResNet-UNet CNN architecture to achieve 82% accuracy in depth map generation of the Solar System's largest canyon.

Comparative Analysis: Traditional ML vs CNN Architectures in Human Detection Systems

Conducted a comprehensive performance analysis comparing traditional Machine Learning algorithms against Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for human detection in digital imagery. The study evaluated key metrics including accuracy, computational efficiency, and real-world applicability across multiple detection methods.
